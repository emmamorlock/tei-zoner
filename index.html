TEI Zoner

This tool allows you to generate TEI <zone> elements by drawing points on an image. It is entirely browser-based (nothing is processed on the server), so it should be very fast. Developed in Firefox, but should work in any modern browser.

How to use:

  • To set points of your shape, click anywhere on the image.
  • To draw your shape, click [Draw Shape] in the bar at the bottom of the screen.
  • If you have only drawn two points, you will get a rectangle. Otherwise, a polygon.

Uses raphael.js, highlight.js and jQuery, and owes a lot to the fine people of StackOverflow (in particular this question).

Info, comments, suggestions: @sparkyc84.
